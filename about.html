    About Us



    The Black Excellence Network is a social enterprise which provides mentorship for black students. The aim of the organisation is to aid black sixth form students in attaining places in top universities and aid personal development through the help and inspiration of their mentors. Mentorship is virtual and safeguarded to protect our mentees and mentors safety whilst ensuring safe and efficient communication. Although at our core we are a mentorship service, we host a range of events and opportunities for our students to help them in anyway possible.
